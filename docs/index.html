About.

Located in Bristol, England. I have worked as a freelance consultant in the past and am always interested in impactful opportunities. This can be from accessibility audits, to wireframing or coding front-end components.

  • High-level accessibility audits to understand areas for improvement, focused on best-practices, WCAG compliance, and practical next steps
  • User experience content writing
  • Wireframing and interactive prototypes
  • Creating design language and component libraries
  • Developing new and implementing new components improvements and bugfixes (or advise the team on the improvements)
  • Conducting user testing and synthesizing data into actionable opportunities

Previously, I have worked in-house in start-ups and small businesses, where I focused on designing and building intuitive front-ends. I have also worked as a graphic and product designer.

My strength has always been in multidisciplinary roles. I excel at performing audits and reviews, engaging in user testing, writing design system libraries, and getting hands-on with front-end development. And in engineering projects I typically take on a double role as ux designer/front-end developer.

Available for

Accessibility & heuristic reviews.


Struggling to manage WCAG compliance as your product scales?

I can do a detailed or high-level accessibility / heuristic review, and based on the outcomes suggest fixes and next steps.

Front-end development.


As a front-end developer and designer I can wireframe, prototype and build products that are user friendly, accessible and scalable.

I can build accessible websites and prototypes, make improvements to existing products, or work to implement new features.

Wireframing & prototyping.


Got some ideas and just want to see what a new feature will look like?

I can create one one-off or batch prototypes using Figma and Adobe CC that you can share with your team or with users to test.

Creating design systems.


I build good looking, user-friendly and responsive design systems using the Atomic Design Methodology. I can run the process from start to finish, and design directly to code.
